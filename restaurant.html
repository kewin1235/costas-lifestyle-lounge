The Kitchen & The Bar

Plates that delight. Drinks that elevate.

At Costa's, food and drink are part of the experience — never an afterthought. Our kitchen serves delicious dishes at prices that welcome everyone, while our bar pours premium spirits, signature cocktails and classics done right.

Pair your meal with a Northern Cape sunset on our rooftop, settle in beneath the stars on our outdoor seating, or enjoy the warm lounge inside — wherever you sit, you're in good hands.
