The Story of Costa's

Where Postmasburg comes to celebrate, connect and indulge — in a setting built for those who value class, comfort and great company.

Our Beginning

Born from a love for lifestyle and good company.

Costa's Lifestyle Lounge began with a simple idea: that Postmasburg deserved a space where elegance, entertainment and exceptional hospitality came together under one roof. From the first guests through the door to the 17,000+ followers we count today, every evening here has been about one thing — celebration.

Tucked into Boichoko on Matsoabane Street, our doors open onto a world of warm lighting, plush seating and the gentle hum of a crowd ready to make memories. Whether you're here for a quiet dinner, a brunch with friends or a private VVIP affair, you'll find Costa's holds a standard the Northern Cape has come to expect.

The Atmosphere

A space designed for moments worth remembering.

Three distinct experiences. One unforgettable venue.

i.

The Lounge

Soft lighting, intimate seating and a sound system that knows when to whisper and when to soar. The lounge is where conversations stretch into the early hours.

ii.

The Restaurant & Bar

A kitchen serving plates that make a Tuesday feel like a Saturday, paired with a bar that pours with care. From cocktails to crisp beers, every drink has a place here.

iii.

The Rooftop

Open skies, a soft Northern Cape breeze and golden-hour drinks. Our rooftop is the venue's crown — perfect for sundowners, celebrations and slow conversations.

iv.

The VVIP Experience

Reserved tables, private booths and a level of service that knows the difference between attention and intrusion. Costa's VVIP is quietly the best seat in the Northern Cape.

v.

Outdoor Seating

For evenings when the weather invites you out, our outdoor seating captures the same quality and atmosphere — under the stars.

vi.

The Calendar

Live music, DJ sets, brunch celebrations, karaoke nights and private hires. There's always something happening at Costa's — and you're always welcome.

Why Guests Return

It's not just a venue — it's a feeling.

  • Genuine Hospitality

    Our team treats every guest like they belong — because they do. From the doorman to the kitchen, hospitality is the heart of what we do.

  • Curated Atmosphere

    Every detail — from the lighting to the music to the menu — is chosen to elevate your evening, not to interrupt it.

  • Northern Cape Standard

    Recognised by Northern Cape News Network as a topnotch entertainment joint that "oozes with class." We work hard to keep that title.

  • Built for Celebrations

    Whether it's intimate or grand, Costa's adapts to the occasion — from brunch socials to corporate events to once-in-a-lifetime parties.
