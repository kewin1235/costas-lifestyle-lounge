Hire The Venue

A space that elevates the occasion.

Costa's Lifestyle Lounge is available for hire for private functions, webinars, conferences, birthdays, milestone celebrations and corporate events. With our restaurant, multiple bars, lounge, rooftop and outdoor seating, we can configure the venue to suit anything from intimate gatherings to large-scale productions.

Our team handles the planning details — catering, drinks, audio-visual and atmosphere — so you can focus on your guests and your moment.
